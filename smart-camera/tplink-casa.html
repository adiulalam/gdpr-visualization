Overview of TP-Link Casa Camera


the TP-Link Kasa are meant to provide user with a video feed indoor which can record and be control via the user. The user can control the camera by downloading the official app on the app store or play store and alternatively, user can link the camera to Amazon Alexa or Google Assistance to control and view it via remotely. The TP-Link KC100 Kasa smart security camera was released in 2019 and the price for the camera is Â£32.99 on Amazon as of 20th of March 2021.

The smart camera uses Wi-Fi to connect to the server as it provides the user with subscription-based backup scheme where the recorded footage is automatically backed up. The camera is both app compatible and smart assistance compatible meaning you can view the recorded footage from Amazon Alexa or the Google Assistance software. The video can be configured to motion detection and provides user with free 2-day recordings or 1 GB of cloud recording. The camera also has audio feed meaning it can both record the video as well as the audio within the specified range.
