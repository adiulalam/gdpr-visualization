Overview of Fire Stick 4K


the Amazon Fire Stick 4K is to provide streaming service for the users to view. User can download many different streaming apps from the store and, in most cases the most popular streaming apps comes preinstalled. The streaming apps may include YouTube, Netflix, Amazon Prime Video etc. Most streaming service requires subscription to view content, which is paid separately. The Amazon Fire Stick 4K was also released in 2020 and the price is Â£49.99 on Amazon as of 20th of March 2021.
The Fire Stick comes in two separate parts, where the first part is the streaming stick, and the second part is the remote. The remote has many different button which includes volume control, play, and pause button, next and previous button, home, and back button etc.
Fire Stick needs to be connected to Wi-Fi for it to work as it comes with a remote which used to navigate throw the menus and options. The Fire Stick needs to be plugged into a HDMI port, this means without a TV or a monitor which has a HDMI port, the Fire Stick will not work. The Fire Stick remote has an Alexa enabled assistant which allows the user to input their voice to search up any request. These can include searching for a specific movie or a tv show in a supported application.
