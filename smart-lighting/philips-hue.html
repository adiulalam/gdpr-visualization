Overview of Philips Hue Bulb


the Philips Hue is to provide control of the lighting which would provide a setting where the user can customize their experience. Philips Hue has many different light bulbs which has different and unique style such as ambiance bulbs, white bulbs, and multi-coloured bulbs. The Philips Hue can be control either with an app or a bridge but however the bridge is separate and cost extra. The Philips Hue generation 3 which is the latest gen was released back in 2016 and the price for white ambiance starter kit is Â£69.99 on Amazon as of 20th of March 2021.

The Philips Hue light bulbs does not directly connect to the internet as it requires the app which can be download from a smartphone, or it can connect to a bridge. By connecting the bulbs to an app or bridge, the user can control it by Bluetooth and alternatively it can be set up and controlled by smart assistance such as Amazon Alexa or Google Assistance.
